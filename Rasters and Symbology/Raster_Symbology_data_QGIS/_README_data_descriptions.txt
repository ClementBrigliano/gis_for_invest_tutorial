
Sample data for the GIS for InVEST tutorial Overview of Coordinate Systems


erosivity_Nepal.tif: Rainfall erosivity raster, used by the SDR model, for our study area in Nepal

LULC_ESA_Legend.csv: Table containing a mapping between land cover codes and descriptions for the European Space Agency land use/land cover data.

LULC_ESA_Nepal.tif: Land use/land cover raster, used by many InVEST models, for our study area in Nepal. See LULC_ESA_Legend.csv for a description of the land cover types.
